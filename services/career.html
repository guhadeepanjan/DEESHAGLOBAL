What we do for you

Career Compass is not motivational talk. It’s a practical process: understand your profile, choose a direction with logic, build a skills plan, and prepare you to perform — on paper and in conversation.

Assessments

Strengths, interests, values, and fit — so your plan matches who you actually are.

1:1 counselling

Structured conversations that turn confusion into clear decisions.

Skills roadmap

Gap analysis + upskilling plan — what to learn, why it matters, and how to do it.

Job readiness

Resume, LinkedIn, interview preparation — aligned to target roles, not generic templates.

The outcome

You’ll leave with a clearer direction, a realistic plan, and a confident narrative — so your next step feels deliberate, not random.

How we work

A simple, premium structure — clarity first, then execution. No overwhelm.

1) Diagnose

Profile, constraints, strengths, and current stage.

2) Decide

Direction choices with reasoning — role families, pathways, and fit.

3) Build

Skills roadmap + proof plan (projects, portfolio, achievements).

4) Position

Resume + LinkedIn narrative aligned to your target roles.

5) Perform

Interview readiness, story clarity, and confidence under pressure.
