About Deesha Global

DEESHA GLOBAL PTE. LTD. is a Singapore-based global services company built at the intersection of people, education, and technology — designed to bridge ambition with execution across borders.

Singapore Hub

Positioned in a leading business and education ecosystem.

India–Asia Bridge

Connecting Indian talent with Asian and global opportunity.

Integrated Platform

Five pillars working together seamlessly.

Technology-enabled

AI-powered solutions with human expertise.

Vision

To be the world’s leading integrated platform empowering educational, professional, and entrepreneurial aspirations globally, responsibly, and sustainably.

Mission

We help people and organizations navigate education, careers, and global opportunities with clarity and confidence — blending knowledge, innovation, and practical execution.

Founder

Shashwati Guha — Founder & Director

When I started Deesha Global, it wasn’t to build just another advisory platform. It was to create something I had personally looked for — clarity in decision-making, honesty in guidance, and systems that respect both people and outcomes.

My journey has taken me across different worlds — engineering, education, global risk and investigations, and leadership development. Each phase shaped how I work: structure without rigidity, empathy without compromise, and governance without fear. Many people struggle not because of a lack of ambition, but because advice is fragmented and decisions are made without a clear framework.

At Deesha Global, we take a long-term view. We believe growth should be intentional, ethical, and sustainable. Whether we’re guiding a student, supporting a career transition, enabling a founder journey, or using technology to improve outcomes, our approach stays consistent — clarity before speed, structure before scale, and trust above all else.
